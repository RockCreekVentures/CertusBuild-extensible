About CertusBuild

CertusBuild is an initiative dedicated to transforming the construction industry through intelligent automation and data-driven insights. Our mission is to empower construction firms and contractors of all sizes to overcome traditional challenges, enhance efficiency, improve profitability, and deliver projects with greater certainty and success.

Our Focus

We are currently focused on developing a cutting-edge SaaS platform that addresses critical pain points in the pre-construction and project management lifecycle, including:

  • Streamlining the analysis of complex bid solicitations.
  • Providing robust and integrated durations and cost estimation.
  • Incorporating advanced uncertainty management and risk analysis.
  • Enhancing collaboration and decision-making for project teams.

The Vision

Our long-term vision is to create a comprehensive ecosystem of tools that leverage artificial intelligence, machine learning, and best-in-class project management principles to set a new standard for excellence in construction technology. We aim to be a trusted partner for construction professionals, helping them navigate the complexities of modern projects and build a more efficient and sustainable future.
