MVP Definition & Research

This section details the research and planning for the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) of the CertusBuild platform, focusing on core features and functionality that address critical pain points in construction management.

MVP Core Components

Durations Estimation
  • Historical data integration
  • AI-powered estimation
  • Activity sequencing tools
  • Schedule visualization
Bid Preparation
  • Document parsing automation
  • Requirements extraction
  • Cost estimation tools
  • Proposal generation
Uncertainty Management
  • Risk assessment tools
  • Uncertainty visualization
  • Scenario modeling
  • Confidence metrics

Research Methodology

Our MVP definition is based on extensive research including:

  • Interviews with construction professionals
  • Analysis of existing solutions and their limitations
  • Evaluation of technological feasibility
  • Assessment of market demand and potential impact

The resulting MVP represents a balanced approach that addresses critical pain points while establishing a foundation for future expansion and enhancement.
